Which Countries Are The Most Popular Travel Destination in 2016?

As world tourism countinues to grow, with so many countries in the world, it's natural to wonder which countries are the most popular travel destination among them all. The graph above shows the top 10 travel destination around the world and their inbound tourism number in 2016.

A bar graph gives a clear comparison to the inbound tourism number among these countries and make it very easy for reader to see.

Source: data.worldbank.org
